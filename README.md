# NHS App Guide — No Photo ID

A simple, plain-English guide for patients registering for GP online services and activating the NHS App — **without needing photo ID**.

Built for patients who find the process confusing, and for social prescribers and support workers to share with the people they work with.

🔗 **[View the live guide](https://kqcworks.github.io/NHS-App-Guide/)**

---

## What it covers

- Requesting online access from your GP surgery
- Receiving your linkage details (linkage key, ODS code, account ID)
- Setting up an NHS login and verifying identity without photo ID
- Linking your GP surgery to the NHS App

## Who it's for

Anyone helping patients get online access to their GP records — including social prescribers, community health workers, carers, and patients themselves.
